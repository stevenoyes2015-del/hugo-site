Functional Analysis

Functional Analysis investigates infinite-dimensional vector spaces and linear operators, providing fundamental tools for understanding differential equations, quantum mechanics, optimization, and other areas where continuous structures play a central role.

Semigroups

Semigroups Research studies algebraic structures with an associative operation, exploring their algebraic, topological, and analytical properties and their applications to dynamics, operator theory, and other areas of mathematics.
